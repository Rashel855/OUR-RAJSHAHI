Rajshahi Tourist Attractions

Discover the historical sites, serene parks, cultural institutions, and other points of interest that make Rajshahi a unique destination.

Historical Sites

Explore the rich history of Rajshahi through its ancient ruins and architectural marvels.

Paharpur Buddhist Vihara (Somapura Mahavihara)

Location: Naogaon (near Rajshahi)

UNESCO World Heritage Site, ancient Buddhist monastery.

Bagha Mosque

Location: Bagha Upazila, Rajshahi

Historic mosque from the Sultanate period.

Puthia Temple Complex

Location: Puthia Upazila, Rajshahi

Group of ancient Hindu temples with intricate terracotta work.

Parks & Recreation

Enjoy the natural beauty and green spaces of Rajshahi.

Shaheed A.H.M. Kamaruzzaman Central Park and Zoo

Location: Padma Garden area, Rajshahi

Popular for family outings, diverse flora and fauna.

Padma Garden

Location: Along the Padma River bank

Scenic riverside park, ideal for sunsets.

Museums & Cultural Sites

Dive into the local culture and heritage of the region.

Varendra Research Museum

Location: Rajshahi University Campus

Oldest museum in Bangladesh, rich collection of artifacts.
